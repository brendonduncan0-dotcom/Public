## DM Notes on Time

### Pacing Guidelines
- Let 1-3 in-game days pass per session during investigation
- Dungeon crawls happen in "real time" (one session = hours in-game)
- Overland travel: Track days precisely
- City exploration: Can be flexible with time

### When to Advance Time
- After long rests
- During travel montages
- During downtime activities
- Between major story arcs

### Time Pressure
Use the calendar to create natural urgency:
- Festivals create deadlines
- Seasons affect travel options
- NPCs have schedules
- Events proceed with or without party
