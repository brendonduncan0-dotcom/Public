## Campaign Rules & House Rules

*Note any special campaign rules, progression mechanics, or house rules being used for this campaign.*

#### Building Your Warband
Before the first mission, each player receives 150pts to spent on their warband. A player may save up to 30pts to be spent later, and they must obey the force organization rules below: 
- **Heroes**: Max 1
- **Copies of Same Unit**: Max 2
- **Most Expensive Unit**: Max 52 points (35%)
- **Total Units**: Max 6
- **Total Models**: Max 10

*Note: The limits above only apply when initially building the warband, there are no limitations to points spent later.*
