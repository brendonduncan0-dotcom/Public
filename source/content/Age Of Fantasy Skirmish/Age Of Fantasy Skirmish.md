Age of Fantasy Skirmish is a free, quick-play tabletop miniature wargame by [One Page Rules (OPR)](https://onepagerules.com/games/age-of-fantasy-skirmish). Players build small warbands and clash in fast-paced battles set in a world of magic, ancient ruins, and warring factions. The rules are deliberately streamlined — the full core rules fit on a single page — making it easy to pick up and play without sacrificing tactical depth.

## About the Game

<br>

**Publisher:** One Page Rules (OPR) — rules are free to download  
**Scale:** Skirmish — typically 5–15 miniatures per side  
**Points:** Standard games run at 150–250pts  
**Table Size:** Recommended 4×4 (we play on 3×3)  
**Core Mechanic:** Roll a d6 against your unit's Quality score to hit, Defence score to survive

Each unit has just two core stats — Quality and Defence — plus weapon profiles and optional special abilities. This keeps the game fast without removing meaningful decisions.

## Quick Reference

### Turn Structure
1. **Initiative** — Both players roll a d6; highest goes first
2. **Activations** — Players alternate activating one unit at a time
3. **Actions** — Each unit can Move + do one Action (Attack, Shoot, Cast Spell, etc.)
4. **Round End** — Check objectives; repeat until the game ends

### Core Dice Rules
- **To Hit:** Roll a d6 per attack die; meet or beat your Quality score
- **To Defend:** Roll a d6 per hit; meet or beat your Defence score
- **Morale:** Units that take casualties may need to take a Morale test

### Key Conditions
- **Stunned** — Unit cannot act; automatically removed if charged
- **Shaken** — Unit must take a Morale test before activating
