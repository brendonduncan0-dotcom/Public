Age of Fantasy Skirmish is a free, quick-play tabletop miniature wargame by [One Page Rules](https://onepagerules.com/games/age-of-fantasy-skirmish). Players build small warbands and clash in fast-paced battles set in a world of magic, ancient ruins, and warring factions. The rules are deliberately streamlined — the full core rules fit on a single page — making it easy to pick up and play without sacrificing tactical depth.

## About the Game

**Publisher:** One Page Rules (OPR) — rules are free to download  
**Scale:** Skirmish — typically 5–15 miniatures per side  
**Points:** Standard games run at 150–250pts  
**Table Size:** Recommended 4×4 (we play on 3×3)  
**Core Mechanic:** Roll a d6 against your unit's Quality score to hit, Defence score to survive

Each unit has just two core stats — Quality and Defence — plus weapon profiles and optional special abilities. This keeps the game fast without removing meaningful decisions.
