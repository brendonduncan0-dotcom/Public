## Description

Giant kobolds are rare mutations that occur when a kobold is exposed to dragon magic or alchemical growth serums. Standing nearly 8 feet tall, these creatures retain the cunning and cowardice of their smaller kin but possess formidable strength. They often serve as shock troops or elite guards in kobold warrens, using their size to intimidate intruders while maintaining the tactical advantages of pack tactics. Despite their increased stature, giant kobolds remain loyal to dragons and often decorate themselves with draconic imagery and trophies.
