# Lore Codex

## Purpose
A living document of world knowledge, historical events, legends, and truths discovered during the campaign. Information marked with ✓ has been discovered by the party; unmarked information is for DM reference only.

## The Crown of Unity

### Common Knowledge (Everyone in Valdoria knows this)
- ✓ A century ago, five kingdoms were united under the Crown of Unity
- ✓ The Crown was shattered in a great betrayal
- ✓ Since then, the kingdoms have been divided and often at war
- ✓ The fragments of the Crown are said to be scattered across Valdoria

### Uncommon Knowledge (Requires research or NPC information)
- The Crown was forged by the five founding monarchs working together
- Each kingdom has a different story about who betrayed whom
- Scholars disagree on whether the Crown was destroyed or deliberately shattered
- Some legends say the Crown had the power to control minds

### Rare Knowledge (Requires significant investigation)
- [ ] The Crown was not a symbol of unity but a magical prison
- [ ] The "betrayal" was actually a coordinated sacrifice by five heroes
- [ ] Each fragment is guarded by a spectral champion
- [ ] The fragments are awakening after a century of dormancy

### Hidden Truth (Discovered through main quest)
- [ ] The Crown imprisoned an entity called the Hollow King
- [ ] The original heroes willingly became immortal guardians
- [ ] Reuniting the Crown could free the Hollow King
- [ ] The royal bloodlines carry part of the sealing ritual in their magic

## Major Historical Events

### The Founding Era (500 years ago)
- Five separate kingdoms constantly at war
- A coalition of monarchs decided to end the conflict
- The Crown of Unity was forged in a ritual combining all five kingdoms' magic

### The Golden Century (400-100 years ago)
- Valdoria experienced unprecedented peace and prosperity
- The Crown passed between kingdoms on a rotating basis
- Great works of magic and architecture were created
- (Hidden truth: This was also when the Hollow King was gathering power)

### The Shattering (100 years ago)
**Official Story**: The five kingdoms turned on each other in a betrayal, and the Crown was destroyed in the resulting battle.

**True Story** (not yet discovered):
- The Hollow King had corrupted the Crown from within
- Five heroes (one from each kingdom) made a desperate plan
- They deliberately shattered the Crown, trapping the Hollow King
- The heroes transformed themselves into eternal guardians
- The kingdoms' leaders agreed to lie about what happened to prevent attempts at reunification

### The Century of Division (100 years ago - present)
- The five kingdoms have been separate and often hostile
- Border skirmishes and trade disputes are common
- Each kingdom tells a different story about the Shattering
- Small wars have erupted but never expanded to full conflict

## The Hollow King

### Legends and Stories
- Said to be an ancient evil sealed away long ago
- Some stories call it a demon, others a fallen god
- Supposedly feeds on conflict and division
- Name varies by culture: The Hollow King, The Empty Throne, The Consuming Shadow

### Actual Nature (DM Knowledge - not yet discovered)
- [ ] Not a demon or god, but the accumulated rage and pain of conquered peoples
- [ ] Was summoned accidentally during the Crown's creation
- [ ] Slowly corrupted the Crown from within over four centuries
- [ ] Seeks not to rule but to unmake—to return everything to void
- [ ] Can only be destroyed by willing unity, not magical binding

## The Veilwalkers

### What is Known
- ✓ Mysterious figures hunting the crown fragments
- ✓ Appear as shadowy humanoids wrapped in darkness
- ✓ Extremely dangerous in combat

### What Can Be Discovered
- [ ] Not human—they're shades, spiritual echoes
- [ ] Were once the servants of the five heroes
- [ ] Corrupted by the binding ritual that trapped the Hollow King
- [ ] Seek to reunite the Crown to free their masters (not understanding this would free the Hollow King)

### Leadership
- [ ] The Veilwalker Prime leads them
- [ ] The Prime is the shade of the first hero who shattered the Crown
- [ ] Can be reasoned with if the party discovers the truth

## Magic and the World

### The Weave of Magic
- Magic in Valdoria is tied to the land itself
- Each kingdom has its own magical tradition
- The Crown tapped into all five traditions simultaneously

### Costs of Great Magic
- The most powerful spells require sacrifice
- The Crown's creation required a sacrifice (unknown what)
- The Shattering transformed the five heroes permanently
- Magic has consequences that extend beyond the caster

## Prophecies and Omens

### The Reunification Prophecy
*An old saying, often dismissed as superstition:*

"When five become one, the prisoner stirs.
When one becomes five, the prison shatters.
Only when many choose as one,
Can the hollow be filled and the king dethroned."

- [ ] The party may discover this prophecy hints at the solution
- [ ] "Many choose as one" suggests the people, not just heroes, must unite
- [ ] The "hollow" being filled might mean healing the Hollow King, not destroying it
