# Kingdom of Ironpeak

## Overview
Carved into and beneath the northern mountains, Ironpeak is a kingdom of miners, smiths, and warriors. Its people value strength, craftsmanship, and honor. The kingdom's wealth comes from its abundant mineral resources and legendary metalwork.

## Geography
- Massive mountain range with cities built into the stone
- Extensive underground tunnel networks
- Rich veins of iron, silver, and rare metals
- Harsh winters, brief summers
- Natural defenses make invasion nearly impossible

## Culture
**Values**: Strength, craftsmanship, loyalty, tradition
**Known For**: Master blacksmiths, dwarven alliances, mountain fortresses
**Stereotypes**: Stubborn, direct, suspicious of outsiders, competitive

## Government
Ruled by [[Queen Aldara the Just]], who took the throne after her father's death in the Border Wars. She's beloved by her people for her fair judgments and military prowess, but her advisors push for aggressive expansion.

## Current Events
- Border tensions with [[Kingdom of Silvermere]] over mining rights
- Secret expeditions to find the "Northern Fragment" of the Crown
- Underground rumors of ancient forges being reactivated
- Diplomatic mission to Thornhaven to claim the discovered fragment

## Connections to Main Plot
- One of the original five kingdoms united by the Crown
- Royal bloodline carries part of the original sealing ritual
- The "Iron Fragment" is hidden somewhere in the deepest mines
- Queen Aldara's grandfather was one of the heroes who shattered the Crown

## Key Locations
- [[Ironhold]] - The capital city, carved into Mount Ironheart
- [[The Deepforges]] - Ancient halls where the Crown was originally forged
- [[Sentinel's Pass]] - Mountain fortress guarding the southern approach

## Notable NPCs
- [[Queen Aldara the Just]] - Current monarch
- [[Master Forgewright Tormund]] - Royal blacksmith who suspects the Crown's secrets
- [[General Krag Ironhammer]] - Military leader, ambitious and aggressive
