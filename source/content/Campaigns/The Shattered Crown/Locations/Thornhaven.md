# Thornhaven

## Description
Perched on the border between three kingdoms, Thornhaven has always been a crossroads of culture, trade, and conflict. The town is built around an ancient thornwood tree so massive that its roots form natural cellars beneath the town square, and its branches provide shade for half the settlement.

## Atmosphere
A nervous energy pervades Thornhaven. Merchants hawk wares in three different languages, mercenaries drink away their worries in crowded taverns, and town guards watch the roads with suspicious eyes. Ever since the fragment appeared, strange occurrences have plagued the town—shadows that move wrong, dreams that feel too real, and a palpable sense that something is watching.

## Key Locations

### The Thornwood Plaza
The heart of town, centered around the ancient tree. Markets fill the square by day, but locals avoid it after dark—strange lights have been seen moving through the tree's branches.

### The Split Coin Tavern
Run by [[Garrick the Many-Tongued]], this establishment serves as neutral ground where information flows as freely as ale. The party will likely stay here initially.

### The Governor's Keep
Captain [[Captain Mira Shadowend]] runs military operations from this converted fortress. She's pragmatic, efficient, and desperate for help dealing with the supernatural threats.

### The Rootvaults
Ancient cellars formed by the thornwood's roots. Recently, a section collapsed, revealing a sealed chamber containing the first crown fragment. Strange creatures now emerge from the depths at night.

### The Whispering Market
A black market operating in the warehouse district. If you need something illegal or magical, [[Vesper the Wanderer]] can probably find it—for the right price.

## Current Events
- Mysterious disappearances in the Rootvaults
- Veilwalker agents have been spotted entering town disguised as merchants
- A diplomatic envoy from each of the three nearest kingdoms has arrived, all demanding the fragment
- The thornwood tree is showing signs of disease for the first time in recorded history
- Refugees from the nearby [[The Weeping Woods]] report that the forest is "waking up"

## Secrets
- The thornwood tree is actually a living seal, planted by the original heroes to guard the hidden fragment
- The Town Council knows about the fragment and are secretly negotiating with all three kingdoms
- Captain Shadowend is a former Crown's Keeper who failed to prevent the fragment's discovery
- The Rootvaults connect to an ancient underground network that spans all of Valdoria

## Plot Hooks
- **The Night Terrors**: Townspeople are experiencing shared nightmares since the fragment was discovered
- **Diplomatic Powder Keg**: The three kingdom envoys are one incident away from open conflict
- **The Thorn's Curse**: The dying thornwood could doom the entire town if not healed
- **Creatures from Below**: Something is definitely prowling the Rootvaults at night

## Connections
- Border of [[Kingdom of Ironpeak]], [[Kingdom of Silvermere]], and [[Kingdom of Ashenvale]]
- Main road leads to [[The Weeping Woods]]
- Rootvaults connect to [[The First Keep]] (unknown to locals)
