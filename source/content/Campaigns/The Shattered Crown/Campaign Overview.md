# The Shattered Crown

## Campaign Hook
A century ago, the Crown of Unity—an artifact that bound the five kingdoms of Valdoria together—was shattered in a catastrophic ritual. Now, pieces of the crown are resurfacing, and whoever reunites them could either restore peace or plunge the realm into eternal darkness.

## Core Themes
- **Broken Trust**: Each kingdom blames the others for the Crown's destruction
- **Hidden History**: The truth of what happened is buried in lies and propaganda
- **Power's Price**: Magic always demands sacrifice
- **Unity Through Adversity**: Only by working together can the kingdoms survive

## Campaign Structure

### Act 1: The Gathering Storm (Levels 1-3)
The party is drawn together by mysterious events in the border town of Thornhaven, where the first crown fragment has appeared. They must navigate local politics, uncover a conspiracy, and survive their first encounter with the Veilwalkers—shadowy figures hunting the fragments.

**Key Locations**: [[Thornhaven]], [[The Weeping Woods]]
**Key NPCs**: [[Captain Mira Shadowend]], [[Vesper the Wanderer]]
**Main Quest**: [[Quest - The Fragment of Thornhaven]]

### Act 2: The Five Kingdoms (Levels 4-6)
With one fragment secured, the party must journey through each of the five kingdoms, learning their version of history and racing against rival factions to find the remaining fragments. Political alliances will be tested, and the party will discover that not everyone wants the Crown restored.

**Key Locations**: [[Kingdom of Ironpeak]], [[The Floating Isles of Aethermoor]], [[The Sunken Archives]]
**Key NPCs**: [[Queen Aldara the Just]], [[The Silent Consortium]], [[Professor Thaddeus Grimward]]
**Main Quests**: [[Quest - The Northern Summit]], [[Quest - Secrets Beneath the Waves]]

### Act 3: The Truth Revealed (Levels 7-8)
The party discovers the shocking truth: the Crown was never meant to unite—it was a prison for an ancient entity called the Hollow King. The kingdoms' founders sacrificed their unity to seal away ultimate destruction. But now, someone wants to break that seal.

**Key Locations**: [[The Shattered Vault]], [[The First Keep]]
**Key NPCs**: [[The Hollow King]], [[Archmagus Elenor the Betrayer]]
**Main Quest**: [[Quest - The Traitor's Legacy]]

### Act 4: The Final Choice (Levels 9-10)
The party must decide: reassemble the Crown and face the consequences, destroy the fragments forever and doom Valdoria to eternal division, or find a third path that no one has considered. The fate of five kingdoms rests on their choice.

**Key Locations**: [[The Crown Chamber]], [[The Heart of the World]]
**Key NPCs**: [[The Five Monarchs]], [[The Veilwalker Prime]]
**Main Quest**: [[Quest - The Reunification]]

## Major Factions

### The Crown's Keepers
Descendants of the original royal guards, dedicated to keeping the fragments separated at all costs.

### The Unification Front
Idealists who believe reuniting the Crown will bring peace, ignorant of its true purpose.

### The Veilwalkers
Mysterious agents serving an unknown master, hunting the fragments with ruthless efficiency.

### The Silent Consortium
A secret society of scholars who know the truth and will do anything to prevent the Crown's reassembly.

## Campaign Secrets
- [ ] The Crown wasn't shattered by war—it was a coordinated sacrifice
- [ ] Each kingdom's royal line carries a fragment of the sealing ritual in their blood
- [ ] The Veilwalkers are actually shades of the original heroes, corrupted by their bargain
- [ ] One of the party members may be descended from the original betrayer
- [ ] The Hollow King isn't evil—it's the cumulative rage of a thousand conquered peoples
